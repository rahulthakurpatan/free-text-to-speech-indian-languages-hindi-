Our Mission • April 24, 2026

The Ecosystem
of Voices.

Look, honestly, we never thought Vāṇī AI would become this big. It all started on a lazy Tuesday afternoon in 2025 when I tried to record a simple Hindi narration and my neighbor's water pump ruined my audio. I felt very much angry. Here's the thing—recording your own voice is a headache if you don't have a big studio.

So, we builds this tool (sorry for my English grammar) to help creators like us. We started with Hindi, but soon creators from across Bharat and then Africa and the Middle East started asking for localized studios. Today, we provides a free text to voice ai ecosystem for 24+ languages.

Maintaining 20+ different language portals is very hard for a small team like us, and sometimes we make mistakes, but we believe that premium technology should be free for everyone. Our goal is simple: You write, we speak. No paisa, no login, just pure creativity. It's just the beginning but...
