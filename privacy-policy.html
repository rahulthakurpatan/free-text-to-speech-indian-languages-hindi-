No Data Collection

Vāṇī AI Studio does NOT collect, store, or log any personal data, scripts, or generated audio files on any external server. Our system is designed to provide a high-performance free text to voice ai experience that remains entirely private. Our architecture is 100% client-side, meaning the conversion happens directly in your browser.

API Key Security

Your Google Gemini API key is stored exclusively in your browser's 'localStorage'. This sensitive information never leaves your device except for direct, encrypted communication with Google's API servers. We do not have access to your keys or your Google AI Studio account.

LocalStorage Usage

We use browser LocalStorage solely for your convenience to save API credentials. This prevents you from re-entering your key every time you visit our free text to voice studio. You have total control over this data and can clear it at any time via the 'Reset Key' button in the interface.

Third-Party Services

This application utilizes the Google Gemini API (Google Cloud) for voice synthesis. While Vāṇī AI remains a free text to voice ai app, the actual generation is processed by Google's infrastructure. Users are encouraged to refer to Google’s Privacy Policy regarding how they handle data processed through their API endpoints.

Cookies & Advertisements

We may use Google AdSense to serve ads to keep the service free. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You may opt out of personalized advertising at any time by visiting Google Ad Settings. We do not use cookies to track your scripts or audio usage.

Commercial Rights

Vāṇī AI does not claim any ownership of the audio files you generate. Since you use your own API key to power the free text to voice ai generator, you retain 100% commercial rights to your content for use on YouTube, Social Media, Podcasts, and any professional projects.
